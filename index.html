Our Story

Born from Love
for Real Food

FoodyVilla started in a small kitchen in Pune with a simple dream — to serve food that feels like a warm hug. Our founder Priya believed that great food isn't just about taste; it's about the joy it brings to every table.

Today we serve thousands of happy customers, but our philosophy hasn't changed: every ingredient is hand-picked, every recipe is tested hundreds of times, and every meal is cooked fresh to order.
